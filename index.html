About Hyderabad Wrist Titans

Hyderabad's Armwrestling Powerhouse

Hyderabad Wrist Titans Team was established in 2023 with a vision to promote the sport of armwrestling in Hyderabad and across Pakistan. Our team consists of dedicated athletes who train rigorously to compete at national and international levels.

We believe in the values of strength, discipline, unity, and sportsmanship. Our mission is to develop world-class armwrestlers while fostering a supportive community of enthusiasts.
